Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs one of the most popular dishes for preparing eggs. It is fast tasty and most importantly, you can prepare it in various ways and the only thing that limits us is the imagination.

Ingredients:

  1. Eggs
  2. Butter
  3. Bacon
  4. Chive

Directions:

  1. Melt butter on a hot pan
  2. Put in bacon and fry until it's brown
  3. We stick eggs and reduce fire from time to time stirring
  4. Put the ready eggs on the plate, season with your favorite spices and sprinkle with the cream

Bon appetit!!

Shakshouka

Dish of Tunisian cuisine. Shakshouka is boiled eggs in tomato sauce. Very filling, perfect for a weekend breakfast. I personally tried once and fell in love. Sausage, salami and bacon perfectly match the meal. Must be served with croutons

Ingredients:

  1. Butter
  2. Eggs
  3. Tomato sauce
  4. Chorizoo/Salami/Bacon/Ham
  5. Onion
  6. Spices: salt, pepper, sweet pepper, smoked pepper, cayenne pepper, Roman cumin

Directions:

  1. Melt butter on a hot pan
  2. Add finely chopped onion and the addition of meat
  3. When the onion and sausage are fried, add the tomato sauce, add spices.
  4. To the boiling sauce, dig eggs (in the middle of the pan make a "nest") reduce the heat and cook under cover for 5 minutes

Bon appetit!!
